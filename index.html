Javier Pérez Cano

PhD Student in Economics

I am a PhD candidate in Economics under the supervision of Gabriel Pérez Quirós in the Alicante University at the FAE department.

My research fields are in Macroeconometrics and Fiscal Policy.

I am interested in the economic impact of government policies, and more specifically, in how their announcement can shape their impact. In my current research, I analyze the impact of fiscal policy announcements, both tax changes and public expenditure measures, on economic activity and cross-border spillover effects, relying on multi-frequency data and novel sentiment indices constructed from newspaper coverage
