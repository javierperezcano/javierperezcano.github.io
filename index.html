Javier Pérez Cano

PhD Student in Economics

I am a PhD candidate in Economics under the supervision of Gabriel Pérez Quirós in the Alicante University at the FAE department.

My research fields are in Macroeconometrics and Fiscal Policy.

I am interested in the economic impact of government policies, and more specifically, in how their announcement can modify their impact. In my latest working paper, we analyze the impact of the announcement of tax and public spending changes on the Spanish economy.
